The Engine Loop

Sense · Orient · Decide · Act

CAGED closes the decision cycle gap by running a continuous operational loop across all seven SPHERED dimensions. Where traditional governance operates in cycles, CAGED operates at the speed of the threat.

01 — Sense

Continuous Ingestion

All SPHERED dimensions simultaneously — cyber, kinetic, cognitive, space. Every intelligence feed, every sensor, every command input. Nothing operates in isolation. Nothing is evaluated in a silo.

›
02 — Orient

Pattern Recognition

Real-time reading across dimensions. A drone strike, a cyber event, a logistics disruption, a provenance anomaly — read together, not separately. The engine asks: what does this mean in context of everything else happening right now?

›
03 — Decide

Signal Not Noise

Command receives what matters at the right echelon, at the right time. Not everything to everyone. The engine surfaces critical information to leadership without overwhelming with operational detail. Need-to-know enforced continuously.

›
04 — Act

Posture Assessment

Continuous dual-posture awareness — defensive and offensive simultaneously. Not a toggle. When the balance must shift, command knows before the window closes. The engine answers the question no current framework can: are we winning or losing this operation right now?

What CAGED Is and Is Not

The Connective Governance Layer

CAGED integrates existing defence systems into a unified continuous governance framework. It is the operational intelligence layer that makes your entire defence apparatus work together coherently — at decision speed.

✗

Not a SIEM replacement

CAGED integrates with your existing security information and event management systems. It orchestrates and governs them — it does not replace them.

✗

Not a single technical product

CAGED is an architectural framework and set of integration patterns. It is the governance logic, not the tooling.

✗

Not a static compliance framework

CAGED does not produce quarterly reports. It produces a continuous operational state — updated at the speed of the threat, not the speed of the audit cycle.

✓

IS the continuous governance layer

The connective framework that integrates intelligence feeds, command systems, domain sensors and operational data into a single live engine.

✓

IS multi-domain by design

The same seven SPHERED dimensions govern across cyber, kinetic, hybrid, cognitive and space — calibrated to the active environment.

✓

IS bidirectional

Defensive and offensive posture assessed simultaneously. The engine knows when the balance must shift and surfaces that to command in real time.
